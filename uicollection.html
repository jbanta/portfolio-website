MARCH 2019
Weather App
I created a weather app design that fulfills my specific needs. I borrowed elements that I like and excluded features I don't use from various existing weather apps designs - I really like the daily/hourly graphs that show temperature and chance of rain.
Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro
JANUARY 2019
404 Page
I wanted to make a unqiue 404 page that centered around a simple illustration related to the idea of being 'lost'. I decided to go with a fake company called KORK with a message in a bottle lost at sea.
Tools used: Adobe Illustrator
DECEMBER 2018
Calculator
I went for a modern mobile calculator design that still had classic elements such as tactile-looking buttons and grayscale-orange color palette.
Tools used: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Artboard Studio
NOVEMBER 2018
Landing Page
I liked the idea of a transforming landing page that switches between a light and dark theme by the click of a button. I thought of different ways this effect could be useful for some kind of product. I came up with made-up antibiotic drink that had two flavors with dark and light values.
Tools used: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Artboard Studio
OCTOBER 2018
Checkout
My goal was to make a checkout screen where the the final page before placing an order included all the information the user entered for easy review. I took advantage of a two column approach so all the information could be viewed with minimal scrolling.
Tools used: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator
OCTOBER 2018
Sign Up
I wanted to make a standard sign-up/log-in as simple as possible that had the feeling of clear skies, oceans, and sea breeze.
Tools used: Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop
