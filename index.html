You3Dit Landing Page Redesign
OBJECTIVE
I was tasked with re-designing and developing You3Dit's landing page to increase turnover rates and land more clients. I worked on improving their online appearance and shifting their branding to target B2B audiences.
THE WORK
See it live here: www.you3dit.com/

I drafted the mockup on Adobe XD and created the graphic images on Adobe Illustrator before starting the development stage. We collected feedback from target clients, B2B companies, and design experts to iterate & optimize our design.

I developed the pages with HTML, CSS, & Javascript to replicate the design mockup complete with animations, button highlighting, and carousels.
DfAM Discussion Cards & Landing Page
ABOUT DfAM CARDS
The You3Dit x MIT cobranded DfAM (Design for Additive Manufacturing) Cards is a discussion tool that encourages discussion within organizations, businesses and teams around Additive Manufacturing innovations. There are 70+ cards among six categories and include various activities that facilitate discussion.
THE DECK
I designed the layout, graphic icons, and the overall look & feel for each card. We are currently going through multiple prototyping iterations for optimization.
LANDING PAGE
I am working on a landing page to give these cards a strong online presence and direct customers to where they can purchase them. This will be a single-scrolling page that summarizes and displays how they can be used complete with examples, lists of features, and images.
